How to Play

Draft the best weather card

1. SpinSpin for a random U.S. region and city using live NWS observations.
2. PickChoose one available variable from that city: temp, dew point, wind, sky, visibility, or pressure.
3. BuildRepeat until all six slots are filled. Each slot can only be drafted once.
4. Re-spinYou get one region re-spin and one city re-spin per game. Broken observations auto-reroll.
5. ScoreEach mode has different perfect ranges and weights. A perfect build scores 100/100.
6. CompeteLog in to save scores, earn XP, play the Daily Challenge, and challenge friends.
